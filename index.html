How to test & HIRO marker
  1. Use Live Server on this folder and open index.html.
  2. Allow camera access.
  3. Open or print the HIRO image: HIRO marker (official)
  4. Submit the form, then point your phone at the marker.
  5. Tap the door (big touch zone) to toggle a transparent front wall.
