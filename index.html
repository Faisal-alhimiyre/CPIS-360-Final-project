Configure your building

Set the footprint and unit mix below, then open the camera to place the model on the official HIRO marker. The entrance is highlighted in amber — tap it (or the large invisible touch zone in front) to turn the outside walls glassy so the colored apartment layout inside is easy to see.

Quick start

  1. Fill every field you care about (hints under each box explain the numbers).
  2. Tap Open camera & build in AR — the next page starts your webcam.
  3. Print or display the marker: HIRO marker (official).
  4. Point your phone at the marker until the building locks on.
  5. Tap the door area to toggle transparent façades and reveal the interior layout.
  6. Use ← Edit building on the camera page to adjust these values.
