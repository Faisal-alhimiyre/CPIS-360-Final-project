Design a building, then walk around it on your desk

This site is for exploring a layout in AR: you set the size of one floor, how many levels you want, and how rooms are arranged. The numbers are real metres (like an architect’s drawing). If you enter a 20 m by 20 m floor, one level covers 20 × 20 = 400 m² — the same multiplication you would use on paper.

Optional: how the next screens work
  1. On the camera page, the model appears on a printed square marker so the phone can keep it steady while you look around.
  2. Tap the door for the cutaway inside view; swipe to rotate the model.
