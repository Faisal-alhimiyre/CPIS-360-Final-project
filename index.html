How AR works on the next pages
  1. After this form you will paint a simple floor plan in a square grid so each room sits where you want it.
  2. Open the camera, allow video, and point at the marker: HIRO marker (official).
  3. Tap the door or the dark front wall to make façades glassy and see inside.
  4. Use spin buttons on the camera page to turn the model; the marker still fixes position in space.
  5. ← Edit building returns here.
