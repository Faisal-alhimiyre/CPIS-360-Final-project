How to test & HIRO marker
  1. Use Live Server on this folder and open index.html.
  2. Fill the form, then tap Generate — the camera starts only then (so you can read the steps first).
  3. Allow camera when the browser asks.
  4. Open or print the HIRO image: HIRO marker (official)
  5. Point your phone at the marker until the building sticks.
  6. Tap the door (big touch zone) to toggle a transparent front wall.
